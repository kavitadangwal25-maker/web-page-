About Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand, also known as Devbhoomi, is famous for its majestic Himalayas, sacred rivers, ancient temples, diverse culture, and breathtaking landscapes. It is home to the Char Dham, national parks, hill stations, adventure activities, and rich Garhwali–Kumaoni heritage.

Popular Tourist Places

Nainital

Lake district of Uttarakhand, famous for Naini Lake and scenic hills.

Mussoorie

The Queen of Hills offering waterfalls and stunning viewpoints.

Rishikesh

Yoga Capital of the World, known for Ganga Aarti & adventure sports.

Char Dham

Badrinath

Dedicated to Lord Vishnu, surrounded by snow peaks.

Kedarnath

Ancient Shiva temple located near Mandakini river.

Gangotri

The origin place of the sacred river Ganga.

Yamunotri

Birthplace of river Yamuna with natural hot springs.
