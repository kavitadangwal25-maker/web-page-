Char Dham of Uttarakhand

The sacred Char Dham Yatra includes Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath and Badrinath — four holy temples nestled in the Himalayas.

Yamunotri

Source of river Yamuna and first stop of the Char Dham Yatra. Surrounded by stunning mountains and hot springs.

Gangotri

Dedicated to Goddess Ganga, located near the origin of river Ganga (Gaumukh).

Kedarnath

One of the holiest Shiva temples located at 11,755 ft surrounded by snow peaks.

Badrinath

Temple of Lord Vishnu, situated between the Nar and Narayan mountains.
