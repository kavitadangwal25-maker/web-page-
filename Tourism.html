Tourism Places in Uttarakhand

Top 20 Tourist Destinations

Nainital

Famous lake city known for boating and scenic hills.

Mussoorie

Queen of Hills with waterfalls and panoramic Himalayan views.

Rishikesh

Yoga Capital of the World, known for rafting and Ganga Aarti.

Haridwar

Spiritual city famous for the divine Ganga Aarti.

Auli

India’s top skiing destination surrounded by snow peaks.

Dehradun

Beautiful valley with caves, waterfalls, and forests.

Jim Corbett

Oldest national park known for tiger safaris.

Kedarnath

Sacred Shiv temple surrounded by the Himalayas.

Badrinath

Holy temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu.

Yamunotri

Source of River Yamuna and hot springs.

Gangotri

Birthplace of the River Ganga.

Valley of Flowers

UNESCO heritage site full of colorful flowers.

Chopta

Mini Switzerland of India, gateway to Tungnath.

Bhimtal

Beautiful lake town, peaceful & clean.

Ranikhet

Calm hill station with pine forests.

Almora

Cultural town famous for temples and sweets.

Kausani

Known for 300 km wide Himalayan views.

Lansdowne

Peaceful army town with clean environment.

Munsiyari

Magnificent view of Panchachuli Peaks.

Tehri Lake

Famous for water sports & scenic views.
