Culture of Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand has a rich and vibrant culture that reflects the traditions of the Garhwali and Kumaoni regions. From folk dances to festivals, food to traditional clothing, the cultural beauty here is simple, pure and deeply connected to nature and spirituality.

Major Festivals

Kumbh Mela

A spiritual gathering held in Haridwar, attracting millions of devotees.

Harela

Festival of greenery and nature, celebrated by planting trees.

Nanda Devi Mahotsav

A cultural festival celebrated with music, dance, and traditional rituals.

Traditional Foods

Bhaang ki Chutney

A delicious flavored chutney made from roasted hemp seeds.

Kafuli

A famous dish made from spinach and fenugreek leaves.

Chainsoo

Traditional protein-rich dish made of black gram dal.

Folk Dance & Music

Jhumeila

Popular Kumaoni folk dance performed during festivals.

Chholiya Dance

A traditional Garhwali dance performed with swords and shields.

Traditional Music

Includes instruments like Dhol, Damau, Ransingha, and Turri.

Traditional Clothing

Women's Attire

Women wear Ghaghra, Odhni, Pichhora, and jewelry like Galobandh.

Men's Attire

Men wear kurta, pyjama, woolen caps and traditional 'Jholi'.
